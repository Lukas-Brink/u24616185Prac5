Biography and Background

Kelly has been in several orphanages and homeless shelters during her childhood. Despite her circumstances, she worked hard at school and managed to secure a bursary for her talent in the arts. She is currently studying BSC Success and some of her entrepreneurial endeavors have already amounted to functional businesses. She hopes to one day become a capitalist powerhouse— the likes of which Elon Musk would pale in comparison to.

Hobbies

Kelly's official statement when asked about her hobbies was the following: "My only hobby is getting to a bag." While this does not tell us much about her interests, it gives us some insight into her character.
