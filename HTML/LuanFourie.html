Biography and Background

Luan Fourie is exactly 167cm tall. It has been this way for as long as he can remember. Despite this, Luan has managed to pull himself up from his despair and achieve mild success in his later years. He spends most of his time watching Vine compilations on a projector they have set up for him in the nursing home.

Hobbies

Luan's hobbies include his mission to find the worst tasting beer in the world (They are currently all tied for first place), collecting energy drink cans and watching Star Wars or Star Trek, whichever of those have the droids.
